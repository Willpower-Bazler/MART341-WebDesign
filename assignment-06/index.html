Tater Tot Casserole

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 onion (yellow or sweet)
  • 8 oz grated cheddar cheese
  • 2 lbs frozen tater tots
  • 8 oz cream of mushroom (1 can)
  • 8 oz frozen veggies (corn, carrots, green beans, broccoli, whatever you want)
  • coconut oil (or alternative, for greasing pan)

Directions

  1. preheat oven to 400 F
  2. bake tater tots for 10 mins, flip and bake another 10 mins
  3. while tater tots are baking, chop up and saute the onion in a pan with a little coconut oil
  4. add ground beef on top of sauteed onion and cook until no pink is left
  5. once tater tots are finished, turn down to 350 F, and grease your casserole dish
  6. mix the onion beef with the cream of mushroom and the veggies and spread in the dish
  7. put half the grated cheese over the mix, then layer the tater tots, then put on the rest of the cheese
  8. put tin foil over the dish
  9. cook for 35 mins with the foil on, then another 10 mins with the foil off
  10. let sit for 5-10 mins
  11. chow!
