“Do not let your fire go out, spark by irreplaceable spark in the hopeless swamps of the not-quite,
the not-yet, and the not-at-all. Do not let the hero in your soul perish in lonely frustration
for the life you deserved and have never been able to reach. The world you desire can be won.
It exists.. it is real.. it is possible.. It is yours.”


“People think that a liar gains a victory over his victim. What I’ve learned is that a lie
is an act of self-abdication, because one surrenders one’s reality to the person to whom one lies,
making that person one’s master, condemning oneself from then on to faking the sort of reality
that person’s view requires to be faked… The man who lies to the world, is the world’s slave from then on…
There are no white lies, there is only the blackest of destruction, and a white lie is the blackest of all.”
